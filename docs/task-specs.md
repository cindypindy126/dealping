## TASK-007: Home Screen

## TASK-008: Search Screen

## TASK-009: CategoryDetail & ProviderDetail Screens
**Goal:** Two complementary detail views.

# HYPOTHESIS CARDS — Casey

## HYP-001: Users Will Register Cards Upfront ⚠️ HIGH RISK
**Hypothesis:** 40%+ of new users register ≥1 card in first session if onboarding is benefit-focused.
**Invalidation:** <25% → pivot to guest mode with sample data.

## HYP-002: Benefit Rate Is Primary Decision Factor ⚠️ MEDIUM RISK
**Hypothesis:** Users tap top-ranked result (by rate) in ≥60% of search interactions.
**Invalidation:** <45% top-tap rate → weighted score model (rate × cap).

## HYP-003: Search > Category as Entry Point ⚠️ MEDIUM-HIGH RISK
**Hypothesis:** ≥65% of benefit-lookup sessions originate from Search (not Category browse) in first 4 weeks.
**Invalidation:** Category ≥40% → restructure IA, expand category grid prominence.
